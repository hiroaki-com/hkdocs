### 2. Service Overview

This site is an information website.

All payments accepted on this site are voluntary donations and do not constitute the sale of goods or services. No physical goods are shipped, and no guaranteed services are provided in exchange for donations.

### 3. Information We Collect

#### Payment Processing Information

When processing a donation, the following information may be collected by our third-party payment processors on their payment pages:

- Name
- Email address
- Credit card information or electronic payment account information
- Other information required by each payment processor

Financial information such as credit card details is never stored on our servers. This information is managed solely by each payment processor.

#### Analytics Information (Google Analytics)

This site uses Google Analytics, an analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data. The collected data is processed anonymously and does not identify individuals.

For more details, please refer to the following:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Analytics Terms of Service](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/terms/us/)

To opt out of Google Analytics data collection, please use the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

#### Server Logs

Access to this site may result in server logs recording your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and similar information. This data does not identify individuals and is used solely for service improvement.

### 4. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

- Processing donation payments
- Understanding and improving service usage
- Preventing fraud and unauthorized access

### 5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not share personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

- When the user has given consent
