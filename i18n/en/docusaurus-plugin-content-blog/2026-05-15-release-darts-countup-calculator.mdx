### When to Use This App

- For basic practice when aiming for a high score alone at your home dartboard.
- For party play with beginners, when you want to compete purely on points without complex rules.
- When you want to instantly load the tool from your browser bookmarks without worrying about your smartphone's storage capacity.

### Common Features

Like our existing games, you can start using it instantly just by accessing the URL from any device: smartphone, tablet, or PC. Since all inputted scores are processed entirely within the browser, it works smoothly and feels almost like a native offline app, without the stress of network latency.

### Features of the Count-Up Tool

"Count-Up" is the most fundamental and straightforward game in darts, where you compete for the total points earned over a set number of rounds (usually 8 rounds, 24 throws total).

To maximize the appeal of this simple rule, we have prepared an intuitive input UI that is easy to operate even while throwing darts. It naturally allows for smooth recording of 3 throws per round, supports input for Singles, Doubles, Triples, and Bulls, and also features real-time display of your current average score.

Whether you are practicing strictly on your own or having fun with a group, you can record and manage scores at a great pace.
